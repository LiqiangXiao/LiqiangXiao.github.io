I am now a PhD. student at Shanghai Jiao Tong University . I got my bachelor's degree of Information Engineering at Xi'an Jiaotong University. I visited Khoury College of Computer Sciences, Northeastern University for one year from 2018 to 2019 under the supervision of Professor Lu Wang.

My research is focused on natural language processing, reinforcement learning and machine learning. More specifically, my work is mostly about text summarization, conversational search, multi-task learning, as well as application of reinforcement learning on NLP.
