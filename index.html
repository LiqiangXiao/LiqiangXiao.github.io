Liqiang Xiao

I obtained my PhD from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and completed my bachelor's degree in Information Engineering at Xi'an Jiaotong University. From 2018 to 2019, I visited the Khoury College of Cmputer Sciences at Northeastern University, where I was mentored by Professor Lu Wang.

My research is focused on LLM alignment, natural language processing, reinforcement learning. More specifically, my work is mostly about text summarization, conversational search, multi-task learning, as well as application of reinforcement learning on NLP.
